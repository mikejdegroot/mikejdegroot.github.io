About hello this is a change V

I'm a Front End web developer based in London.

I love developing, producing music and being creative.

I grew up in Brighton, did a Music Tech degree at London College of Music and studied Web Development at a General Assembly bootcamp course.

I love creating beautifully styled, useful projects from start to finish and try to combine my passions of music and developing as much as possible.

V

General Assembly

In the summer of 2017 I joined a General Assembly Web development immersive course to learn to code.

The GA immersive opened my eyes to the world of coding and has shown me how creative and exciting being a developer can be.

The feeling of solving several small tasks and problems in order to build a really beautiful and useful product is one which I have not come across in anything else I have ever done and is what I love the most about coding.

GA showed me that I not only had the ability to become a developer, but that I could also have a lot of fun doing it.
